Research Experience

EEG-Music Research Platform

Communication Capstone | March 2025 - June 2025 | Advisor: Nilam Ram

Built React/D3.js platform processing 197 experimental conditions across 44 studies, reducing research analysis time by 80%. The tool aggregates data from EEG studies related to music perception and cognition, providing intuitive visualization of temporal trends in research methods and enabling detailed filtering across multiple dimensions of study design.

This project addresses the challenge of comparing and synthesizing findings across diverse methodological approaches in neuroscience and music research, facilitating discovery of patterns and relationships between experimental designs, musical stimuli, and EEG methodologies.
